About Us

Our Story

Discover the passion and philosophy behind Serenity Spa.

A Vision of Wellness

Founded in 2009 by wellness visionary Elena Hartley, Serenity Spa began with a simple belief: true relaxation is essential for modern living.

After 20 years working in luxury hospitality across Europe and Asia, Elena noticed a gap in the market for spas that combined world-class treatments with genuine, heartfelt care.

Today, Serenity Spa has grown from a small treatment room to a full wellness destination, but our commitment to personalized, transformative experiences remains unchanged.
