More than a haircut

A trip to Sutton Court should feel like a proper escape. Exposed brickwork, warm Edison lighting, buttoned leather seating and industrial detailing set the tone the moment you walk through the door.

Grab a seat, relax on the sofa and let our barbers take care of the rest — whether it's a quick tidy-up or the full treatment, you'll leave looking and feeling your best.
