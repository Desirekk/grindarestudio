Why I'm Building This

I've played mobile RPGs that punish you for having a life outside the game, and others that let whales dominate. I wanted something in between ‚Äî a game that respects your time and rewards skill, not spending.

Light & Shadow is built for both dedicated grinders and casual players. Daily energy limits keep things fair, while deep progression systems reward those who engage more. No one gets left behind, and no one needs to spend money to compete.

My Goal: Transition to full-time development and build a small team of specialists (artists, animators, sound designers, QA) to transform this working prototype into a polished, market-ready game.
